FDSV - Fin Deflection Stablized Vechicle 

-This project is about controlling a vechicle in the air once it is dropped from a certain height. Near the end of it's height, a pair of deployable legs are extended out to prevent the rocket from completely crashing. 

Computer: Raspberry Pi Pico

IMU: IMU6050

Servos: SG90s

Battery: 3.3v Small Drone Battery

Power Amplifier: 3.3v(prongs) to 5v (USB-OUT) + MicroUSB Cable
